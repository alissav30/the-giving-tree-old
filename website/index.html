About theGivingTree

Our mission is to help donors donate easily and effectively to the causes they care about and track their donations over time.

From finding where to donate, to which charities are most in need, and then keeping track of these donations until tax season rolls around, giving is filled with pitfalls for the average donor. We want to create a platform that takes the guesswork out of donating by automatically identifying high-impact nonprofits that users can donate to, letting them set up recurring donations, and managing donations long-term.
